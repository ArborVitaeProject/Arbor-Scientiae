Major Arcana

The Fool

Uranus

Uranus represents sudden changes, innovation, and the breaking of conventions.

Air

Aleph (א) - Ox

Path 11 - Between Kether (Crown) and Chokmah (Wisdom)

Lamentations 1:1 - How lonely sits the city that was full of people! How like a widow has she become, she who was great among the nations! She who was a princess among the provinces has become a slave.

The Fool represents new beginnings, spontaneity, and the embrace of the unknown. It signifies a fresh start and a leap of faith.

The Magician

Mercury

Mercury represents communication, intellect, and the power of the mind.

Bet (ב) - House

Path 12 - Between Kether (Crown) and Binah (Understanding)

Lamentations 1:2 - She weeps bitterly in the night, with tears on her cheeks; among all her lovers she has none to comfort her; all her friends have dealt treacherously with her; they have become her enemies.

The Magician symbolizes manifestation, resourcefulness, and the power of will. It signifies the ability to use one's talents and skills to achieve goals.

The High Priestess

Moon

The Moon represents emotions, intuition, and the subconscious.

Gimel (ג) - Camel

Path 13 - Between Kether (Crown) and Tiphareth (Beauty)

Lamentations 1:3 - Judah has gone into exile because of affliction and hard servitude; she dwells now among the nations, but finds no resting place; her pursuers have all overtaken her in the midst of her distress.

The High Priestess represents intuition, mystery, and the unconscious mind. It signifies inner wisdom and the exploration of one's inner world.

The Empress

Venus

Venus represents love, beauty, and the nurturing aspects of life.

Dalet (ד) - Door

Path 14 - Between Chokmah (Wisdom) and Binah (Understanding)

Lamentations 1:4 - The roads to Zion mourn, for none come to the festival; all her gates are desolate; her priests groan; her virgins have been afflicted, and she herself suffers bitterly.

The Empress symbolizes fertility, abundance, and nurturing. It signifies creativity, growth, and the natural world.

The Emperor

Aries

Aries represents courage, initiative, and the assertive drive to lead.

He (ה) - Window

Path 15 - Between Chokmah (Wisdom) and Tiphareth (Beauty)

Lamentations 1:5 - Her foes have become the head; her enemies prosper, because the Lord has afflicted her for the multitude of her transgressions; her children have gone away, captives before the foe.

The Emperor represents authority, structure, and control. It signifies leadership, power, and the establishment of order.

The Hierophant

Taurus

Taurus represents stability, practicality, and a connection to tradition.

Vav (ו) - Hook or Nail

Path 16 - Between Chokmah (Wisdom) and Chesed (Mercy)

Lamentations 1:6 - From the daughter of Zion all her majesty has departed. Her princes have become like deer that find no pasture; they fled without strength before the pursuer.

The Hierophant symbolizes tradition, spirituality, and conformity. It signifies religious beliefs, education, and the search for spiritual guidance.

The Lovers

Gemini

Gemini represents duality, communication, and the balance of opposites.

Zayin (ז) - Sword or Weapon

Path 17 - Between Binah (Understanding) and Tiphareth (Beauty)

Lamentations 1:7 - Jerusalem remembers in the days of her affliction and wandering all the precious things that were hers from days of old. When her people fell into the hand of the foe, and there was none to help her, her foes gloated over her; they mocked at her downfall.

The Lovers symbolizes love, harmony, and the choices we make in relationships. It signifies union, partnership, and the alignment of values.

The Chariot

Cancer

Cancer represents emotional drive, determination, and the quest for security.

Chet (ח) - Fence

Path 18 - Between Binah (Understanding) and Geburah (Severity)

Lamentations 1:8 - Jerusalem sinned grievously; therefore she became filthy; all who honored her despise her, for they have seen her nakedness; she herself groans and turns her face away.

The Chariot represents determination, control, and victory. It signifies the ability to overcome obstacles and achieve goals through willpower and discipline.

Strength

Leo

Leo represents courage, self-expression, and inner strength.

Tet (ט) - Serpent or Basket

Path 19 - Between Chesed (Mercy) and Geburah (Severity)

Lamentations 1:9 - Her uncleanness was in her skirts; she took no thought of her future; therefore her fall is terrible; she has no comforter. "O Lord, behold my affliction, for the enemy has triumphed!"

Strength represents courage, patience, and inner power. It signifies the ability to remain calm and composed in the face of adversity.

The Hermit

Virgo

Virgo represents introspection, analysis, and the search for truth.

Yod (י) - Hand

Path 20 - Between Chesed (Mercy) and Tiphareth (Beauty)

Lamentations 1:10 - The enemy has stretched out his hands over all her precious things; for she has seen the nations enter her sanctuary, those whom you forbade to enter your congregation.

The Hermit represents solitude, wisdom, and the search for inner guidance. It signifies the need for introspection and spiritual enlightenment.

Wheel of Fortune

Jupiter

Jupiter represents expansion, luck, and the cycles of life.

Kaf (כ) - Palm of the Hand

Path 21 - Between Chesed (Mercy) and Netzach (Victory)

Lamentations 1:11 - All her people groan as they search for bread; they trade their treasures for food to revive their strength. "Look, O Lord, and see, for I am despised."

Wheel of Fortune represents cycles, fate, and the forces of destiny. It signifies changes in life and the ups and downs of fortune.

Justice

Libra

Libra represents balance, fairness, and the law of cause and effect.

Lamed (ל) - Goad or Staff

Path 22 - Between Geburah (Severity) and Tiphareth (Beauty)

Lamentations 1:12 - "Is it nothing to you, all you who pass by? Look and see if there is any sorrow like my sorrow, which was brought upon me, which the Lord inflicted on the day of his fierce anger."

Justice represents fairness, truth, and law. It signifies the importance of making balanced decisions and accepting the consequences of actions.

The Hanged Man

Neptune

Neptune represents sacrifice, intuition, and spiritual enlightenment.

Water

Mem (מ) - Water

Path 23 - Between Geburah (Severity) and Hod (Splendor)

Lamentations 1:13 - "From on high he sent fire; into my bones he made it descend; he spread a net for my feet; he turned me back; he has left me stunned, faint all the day long."

The Hanged Man represents suspension, letting go, and seeing things from a new perspective. It signifies the need for sacrifice and surrender to gain insight.

Death

Scorpio

Scorpio represents transformation, endings, and the cycle of death and rebirth.

Nun (נ) - Fish

Path 24 - Between Tiphareth (Beauty) and Netzach (Victory)

Lamentations 1:14 - "My transgressions were bound into a yoke; by his hand they were fastened together; they were set upon my neck; he caused my strength to fail; the Lord gave me into the hands of those whom I cannot withstand."

Death represents transformation, endings, and the embrace of change. It signifies the end of a cycle and the beginning of a new one.

Temperance

Sagittarius

Sagittarius represents balance, moderation, and the quest for higher knowledge.

Samech (ס) - Prop or Support

Path 25 - Between Tiphareth (Beauty) and Yesod (Foundation)

Lamentations 1:15 - "The Lord rejected all my mighty men in my midst; he summoned an assembly against me to crush my young men; the Lord has trodden as in a winepress the virgin daughter of Judah."

Temperance represents balance, patience, and synergy. It signifies the blending of different elements to create harmony and balance in life.

The Devil

Capricorn

Capricorn represents ambition, materialism, and the potential for self-imposed limitations.

Ayin (ע) - Eye

Path 26 - Between Tiphareth (Beauty) and Hod (Splendor)

Lamentations 1:16 - "For these things I weep; my eyes flow with tears; for a comforter is far from me, one to revive my spirit; my children are desolate, for the enemy has prevailed."

The Devil represents temptation, bondage, and materialism. It signifies the challenges of overcoming addictions, dependencies, and self-imposed limitations.

The Tower

Mars

Mars represents conflict, destruction, and sudden upheaval.

Pe (פ) - Mouth

Path 27 - Between Netzach (Victory) and Hod (Splendor)

Lamentations 1:17 - Zion stretches out her hands, but there is none to comfort her; the Lord has commanded against Jacob that his neighbors should be his foes; Jerusalem has become a filthy thing among them.

The Tower represents sudden change, upheaval, and the breaking down of old structures. It signifies the need for destruction of outdated beliefs to pave the way for new growth.

The Star

Aquarius

Aquarius represents hope, inspiration, and the pursuit of a better future.

Tsadi (צ) - Hook or Fishhook

Path 28 - Between Netzach (Victory) and Yesod (Foundation)

Lamentations 1:18 - "The Lord is in the right, for I have rebelled against his word; but hear, all you peoples, and see my suffering; my young women and my young men have gone into captivity."

The Star represents hope, inspiration, and spiritual guidance. It signifies the renewal of faith and the promise of a brighter future.

The Moon

Pisces

Pisces represents dreams, intuition, and the exploration of the unconscious.

Qof (ק) - Back of the Head or Eye of a Needle

Path 29 - Between Netzach (Victory) and Malkuth (Kingdom)

Lamentations 1:19 - "I called to my lovers, but they deceived me; my priests and elders perished in the city, while they sought food to revive their strength."

The Moon represents illusion, intuition, and the unconscious mind. It signifies the journey through uncertainty and the exploration of deeper truths.

The Sun

Sun

The Sun represents clarity, vitality, and success.

Resh (ר) - Head

Path 30 - Between Hod (Splendor) and Yesod (Foundation)

Lamentations 1:20 - "Look, O Lord, for I am in distress; my stomach churns; my heart is wrung within me, because I have been very rebellious. In the street the sword bereaves; in the house it is like death."

The Sun represents positivity, success, and abundance. It signifies the achievement of goals and the joy of being alive.

Judgement

Pluto

Pluto represents transformation, rebirth, and the uncovering of hidden truths.

Fire

Shin (ש) - Tooth

Path 31 - Between Hod (Splendor) and Malkuth (Kingdom)

Lamentations 1:21 - "They heard my groaning, yet there is no one to comfort me. All my enemies have heard of my trouble; they are glad that you have done it. You have brought the day you announced; now let them be as I am."

Judgement represents reflection, reckoning, and the realization of higher consciousness. It signifies the assessment of past actions and the call to a higher purpose.

The World

Saturn

Saturn represents completion, discipline, and the material world.

Tav (ת) - Mark or Cross

Path 32 - Between Yesod (Foundation) and Malkuth (Kingdom)

Lamentations 1:22 - "Let all their evildoing come before you, and deal with them as you have dealt with me because of all my transgressions; for my groans are many, and my heart is faint."

The World represents completion, achievement, and fulfillment. It signifies the successful conclusion of a journey and the realization of one's goals.

Wands (Fire)

Ace of Wands

Fire

The Ace of Wands symbolizes new beginnings, inspiration, and the potential for growth. It represents a burst of creative energy and the drive to embark on new ventures or projects.

Two of Wands

Mars in Aries

The Two of Wands represents planning and decision-making. It signifies the need to take bold steps, make choices, and strategize for future success, indicating the initial stages of progress.

Mars in Aries reflects the pioneering and assertive energy required to make decisions and take action. Mars, the planet of action and drive, is well-placed in Aries, a sign known for its boldness and initiative. This combination encourages taking the lead and making assertive moves.

Three of Wands

Sun in Aries

The Three of Wands signifies foresight, expansion, and exploration. It suggests that your plans are beginning to take shape, and you are looking forward to new horizons and opportunities.

Sun in Aries highlights the confidence and leadership needed to explore new opportunities. The Sun represents the self and vitality, while Aries brings enthusiasm and a pioneering spirit. This combination emphasizes the readiness to expand and take on new ventures with confidence.

Four of Wands

Venus in Aries

The Four of Wands denotes celebration, harmony, and community. It represents a time of joy and satisfaction, often associated with milestones, achievements, and gatherings with loved ones.

Venus in Aries reflects the joy of new beginnings and the celebration of achievements. Venus, the planet of love and harmony, in the bold sign of Aries suggests a time when relationships and social bonds are energized, leading to festive and joyous occasions.

Five of Wands

Saturn in Leo

The Five of Wands represents conflict, competition, and struggle. It signifies challenges and obstacles that require effort and determination to overcome, often involving interpersonal dynamics.

Saturn in Leo highlights the discipline needed to manage conflicts and competition. Saturn, the planet of challenges and structure, in the expressive sign of Leo indicates the need to balance personal desires with responsibility, often through competitive or challenging situations.

Six of Wands

Jupiter in Leo

The Six of Wands signifies victory, recognition, and triumph. It represents public acknowledgment of your efforts and achievements, bringing a sense of pride and accomplishment.

Jupiter in Leo reflects the expansion and recognition of one's talents and efforts. Jupiter, the planet of growth and abundance, in the proud sign of Leo suggests that success and recognition are achieved through creativity, leadership, and generosity.

Seven of Wands

Mars in Leo

The Seven of Wands denotes defense, perseverance, and maintaining control. It suggests standing your ground and asserting yourself in the face of challenges and competition.

Mars in Leo emphasizes the assertiveness and courage needed to defend one's position. Mars, the planet of action and aggression, in the bold and confident sign of Leo suggests a strong, determined approach to overcoming obstacles and standing up for oneself.

Eight of Wands

Mercury in Sagittarius

The Eight of Wands represents rapid action, movement, and swift change. It indicates that events are unfolding quickly, and decisions or actions will soon bring significant progress.

Mercury in Sagittarius highlights quick thinking and communication, leading to swift progress. Mercury, the planet of communication and travel, in the adventurous sign of Sagittarius suggests a period of rapid development and the exchange of ideas that lead to forward movement.

Nine of Wands

Moon in Sagittarius

The Nine of Wands signifies resilience, determination, and courage. It represents the strength to keep going despite challenges, reflecting perseverance and the readiness to defend your position.

Moon in Sagittarius emphasizes emotional resilience and the quest for meaning. The Moon, representing emotions and inner strength, in the optimistic sign of Sagittarius suggests the inner fortitude and belief needed to persevere through difficulties.

Ten of Wands

Saturn in Sagittarius

The Ten of Wands denotes burden, responsibility, and hard work. It suggests that you are carrying a heavy load and may need to delegate or find ways to lighten your workload to avoid burnout.

Saturn in Sagittarius reflects the heavy responsibilities and the quest for purpose. Saturn, the planet of discipline and hard work, in the philosophical sign of Sagittarius suggests a period where one's beliefs and goals require significant effort and perseverance.

Page of Wands

Earth of Fire

The Page of Wands symbolizes enthusiasm, exploration, and a free spirit. It represents youthful energy, curiosity, and the willingness to embark on new adventures with optimism and excitement.

Earth of Fire represents the grounding of creative energy. This combination suggests a practical approach to new ideas and endeavors, balancing enthusiasm with a solid foundation and realistic planning.

Knight of Wands

Air of Fire

The Knight of Wands represents energy, passion, and adventure. It signifies a dynamic and impulsive approach to life, characterized by a desire for action, travel, and the pursuit of goals.

Air of Fire emphasizes the quick and dynamic expression of energy. This combination highlights the Knight's impulsive and adventurous nature, driven by intellectual curiosity and a desire for excitement.

Queen of Wands

Water of Fire

The Queen of Wands signifies confidence, independence, and determination. She embodies a charismatic and inspiring presence, capable of leading others and achieving success through creativity and passion.

Water of Fire represents the emotional nurturing of creative energy. This combination suggests a compassionate and intuitive approach to leadership, blending passion with understanding and empathy.

King of Wands

Fire of Fire

The King of Wands represents leadership, vision, and authority. He is a powerful and confident figure who takes charge, inspiring others with his ambition, strategic thinking, and entrepreneurial spirit.

Fire of Fire emphasizes pure, unrestrained energy and leadership. This combination highlights the King's strong, dynamic, and authoritative nature, driven by a clear vision and unwavering determination.

Ace of Pentacles

Earth

The Ace of Pentacles symbolizes new beginnings in the material world, such as financial opportunities, prosperity, and the manifestation of goals. It represents the potential for growth and the foundation of future success.

Two of Pentacles

Jupiter in Capricorn

The Two of Pentacles represents balance, adaptability, and the juggling of responsibilities. It signifies the need to manage multiple aspects of life with grace and flexibility.

Jupiter in Capricorn emphasizes the expansion and growth through discipline and hard work. Jupiter, the planet of abundance and growth, in the ambitious sign of Capricorn suggests the need to balance opportunities with responsibilities to achieve long-term success.

Three of Pentacles

Mars in Capricorn

The Three of Pentacles signifies teamwork, collaboration, and skilled craftsmanship. It represents the successful integration of talents and efforts towards a common goal.

Mars in Capricorn highlights the drive and determination to achieve success through hard work. Mars, the planet of action and energy, in the disciplined sign of Capricorn suggests the focused and strategic effort needed to build and accomplish significant projects.

Four of Pentacles

Sun in Capricorn

The Four of Pentacles represents stability, security, and control over material resources. It signifies the desire to maintain and protect one's possessions and wealth.

Sun in Capricorn emphasizes the importance of personal achievement and material success. The Sun, representing the self, in the ambitious sign of Capricorn suggests a strong focus on establishing and maintaining a secure and successful position in the material world.

Five of Pentacles

Mercury in Taurus

The Five of Pentacles signifies hardship, financial loss, and insecurity. It represents a time of struggle and the need to seek support and find ways to recover from difficulties.

Mercury in Taurus highlights the need for practical thinking and communication in times of financial stress. Mercury, the planet of communication, in the stable and practical sign of Taurus suggests the importance of clear thinking and resourcefulness in overcoming material challenges.

Six of Pentacles

Moon in Taurus

The Six of Pentacles represents generosity, charity, and the balance of giving and receiving. It signifies the importance of sharing wealth and resources to create harmony and support within the community.

Moon in Taurus emphasizes the emotional satisfaction derived from material stability and generosity. The Moon, representing emotions, in the nurturing sign of Taurus suggests a deep sense of fulfillment in providing and receiving support and comfort.

Seven of Pentacles

Saturn in Taurus

The Seven of Pentacles signifies patience, assessment, and long-term planning. It represents the need to evaluate progress and make necessary adjustments to achieve sustained growth and success.

Saturn in Taurus highlights the importance of patience and perseverance in achieving material goals. Saturn, the planet of discipline and responsibility, in the steady sign of Taurus suggests that careful planning and consistent effort are essential for long-term success.

Eight of Pentacles

Sun in Virgo

The Eight of Pentacles represents diligence, craftsmanship, and skill development. It signifies the dedication to honing one's abilities and the pursuit of excellence in one's work.

Sun in Virgo emphasizes the importance of attention to detail and perfection in one's work. The Sun, representing the self, in the meticulous sign of Virgo suggests a strong focus on improving skills and achieving high standards through hard work and dedication.

Nine of Pentacles

Venus in Virgo

The Nine of Pentacles signifies self-sufficiency, luxury, and financial independence. It represents the rewards of hard work and the enjoyment of a comfortable and prosperous life.

Venus in Virgo highlights the appreciation of beauty and comfort through practical means. Venus, the planet of love and beauty, in the detail-oriented sign of Virgo suggests that true satisfaction and luxury are achieved through careful planning and diligent effort.

Ten of Pentacles

Mercury in Virgo

The Ten of Pentacles represents wealth, legacy, and family heritage. It signifies the culmination of material success and the establishment of a lasting foundation for future generations.

Mercury in Virgo emphasizes the importance of communication and organization in achieving lasting success. Mercury, the planet of communication, in the practical sign of Virgo suggests that detailed planning and effective management are key to creating and maintaining wealth and legacy.

Page of Pentacles

Earth of Earth

The Page of Pentacles symbolizes ambition, learning, and the beginning of new ventures. It represents the exploration of new opportunities and the willingness to work hard to achieve goals.

Earth of Earth represents the practical and grounded expression of ambition and diligence. This combination suggests a focused and methodical approach to achieving success, emphasizing the importance of practicality and perseverance.

Knight of Pentacles

Air of Earth

The Knight of Pentacles represents responsibility, reliability, and hard work. It signifies a methodical and diligent approach to achieving goals, with a focus on practical and long-term success.

Air of Earth emphasizes the intellectual and communicative aspects of practical work. This combination suggests that the Knight's success comes from a balanced approach, integrating careful planning and clear communication with persistent effort.

Queen of Pentacles

Water of Earth

The Queen of Pentacles signifies nurturing, practicality, and financial security. She embodies a balanced approach to material and emotional well-being, providing support and stability to others.

Water of Earth represents the compassionate and nurturing expression of practicality and security. This combination highlights the Queen's ability to create a harmonious and prosperous environment through empathy and careful management of resources.

King of Pentacles

Fire of Earth

The King of Pentacles represents authority, abundance, and financial mastery. He is a successful and reliable leader, capable of creating and sustaining wealth and prosperity.

Fire of Earth emphasizes the dynamic and authoritative expression of material success. This combination highlights the King's ability to inspire and lead others to achieve prosperity through a combination of vision, determination, and practical wisdom.
